Save nature with us

When we use the gifts of nature, shouldn't we take care of their preservation. Unfortunately, human activity is often aimed at destruction. 
We build plants and factories that poison the world around us. But a good owner 
will never allow his house to be destroyed. 
So people should not seek to destroy, but to manage the cycles of nature. 
And this is possible if we study nature and certainly love it.
